## Key KPIs Explained
- Recency: Months since the last transaction
- Lifespan: Duration between first and last transaction
- Average Order Value (AOV): Revenue per order
- Average Monthly Spend / Revenue: Revenue normalized over time

## Example Insights
- Identified high-value customers based on spending and activity duration
- Highlighted products with improving or declining year-over-year performance
- Revealed product categories contributing the largest share of total revenue
